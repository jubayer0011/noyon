About

I'm a science teacher by profession, but a lifelong learner at heart. Teaching isn’t just a job to me — it’s a calling. I believe education should spark curiosity, encourage critical thinking, and nurture a love for learning that lasts a lifetime. I find joy in simplifying complex ideas and making science come alive for my students. My goal is not just to teach, but to inspire — to help young minds see the world with wonder, logic, and compassion.Outside the classroom, I love traveling, experiencing new cultures, and learning from the world itself. I believe that true growth comes from exploring the unknown and thinking beyond boundaries. I value open minds, honest hearts, and the courage to ask “why. Always learning. Always growing. Always grateful.
